West Virginia Grouse Mapper

The Appalachian Highlands of West Virginia represent the southernmost extent of the range of the Ruffled Grouse (Bonasa umbellus), which extends north across much of Canada and Alaska.

This application displays all major public lands in West Virginia and provides insight on where these lands overlap with optimal grouse habitat. Please confirm hunting regulations in any particular unit prior to hunting.
